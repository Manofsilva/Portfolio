About Me...

Hello! I am a full-stack web developer with an educational background in Foreign Lamguages & Literatures. I am a lifelong student, constantly analyzing and utilizing current and popular technologies while also studying and implementing new and upcoming methods. I specialize and enjoy frontend development, being especially proficient in Bootstrap 4 and currently polishing my ReactJS skills.
